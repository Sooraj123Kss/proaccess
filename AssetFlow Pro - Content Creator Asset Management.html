Legal Best Practices

✓

Always Save License Documentation

Keep records of where you found each asset and what license it uses.

⚠️

Check for Trademark Issues

Even royalty-free assets can contain trademarked logos or products.

👥

Consider Model Releases

Photos with identifiable people may need model releases for commercial use.

📝

Provide Proper Attribution

When required, include creator name, source, and license information.

Platform-Specific Guidelines

YouTube Guidelines

  • Use only verified royalty-free music to avoid copyright strikes
  • Credit sources in video descriptions when required
  • Avoid using assets that may trigger Content ID claims
  • Consider YouTube's monetization policies for commercial use

Instagram Guidelines

  • Ensure commercial use rights for business accounts
  • Be mindful of branded content disclosure requirements
  • Credit creators in captions when attribution is required
  • Consider Instagram's terms for promotional content

Website Guidelines

  • Include attribution pages for credited assets
  • Ensure commercial licenses for business websites
  • Optimize images while maintaining license compliance
  • Document asset sources for legal audit trails
